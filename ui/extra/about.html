About Us

Welcome to Lingonberry, a project dedicated to breaking down language barriers through innovative real-time speech translation technology. Our mission is to foster seamless communication and understanding in a globalized world, making virtual interactions more inclusive and productive.

This project originated as a Bachelor's thesis, focusing on the design, implementation, and evaluation of a robust real-time speech translation system. We leverage state-of-the-art open-source models for Speech-to-Text (STT), Machine Translation (MT), and Text-to-Speech (TTS) to deliver low-latency, high-quality translations.

Our Vision

We envision a world where language is no longer a barrier to connection and collaboration. By providing accessible and efficient translation tools, we aim to empower individuals and organizations to communicate freely, regardless of their native tongue.

Key Technologies

  • FastAPI: For a high-performance, asynchronous backend.
  • Faster-Whisper: Optimized Speech-to-Text for accurate transcription.
  • CTranslate2 with Opus-MT: Efficient Neural Machine Translation.
  • Piper TTS & Coqui-TTS: For natural-sounding speech synthesis and advanced voice cloning.
  • WebRTC VAD: For intelligent Voice Activity Detection.

We are committed to continuous improvement and exploring new advancements in AI and natural language processing to enhance our system's capabilities and user experience.
